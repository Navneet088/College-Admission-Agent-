[DOCUMENT: ACADEMIC ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA]
For B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), applicants must have completed Class 12 (High School) with a minimum aggregate score of 75% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM). English marks must be passed but are excluded when calculating the core PCM aggregate score. For the BCA program, students from Science, Commerce, or Arts backgrounds are eligible provided they had Mathematics or Computer Science as a core subject in Class 12. Diploma holders who completed a 3-year AICTE-approved diploma are eligible for direct lateral entry into the second year (3rd semester) of B.Tech, requiring a minimum of 60% aggregate marks in their diploma exams. The minimum aggregate score required for postgraduate MBA program eligibility is 50% in any graduation discipline from a recognized university.

[DOCUMENT: FEE STRUCTURE AND PAYMENT POLICIES]
The tuition fee for the B.Tech Computer Science program is ₹1,25,000 per semester. The one-time registration admission fee is ₹10,000 and is completely non-refundable upon seat cancellation. Semester examination fees are ₹2,500 and laboratory insurance charges are ₹1,500, billed every semester. A refundable library and campus safety caution deposit of ₹5,000 is collected at the time of entry. Annual hostel accommodation charges are ₹90,000, which strictly includes three mess meals a day. College semester fees can be paid via Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card, Demand Draft (DD), or UPI. The fine rate for late payment of semester fees after the due date is ₹200 per day. Campus transport/bus facility is optional and charges an extra ₹15,000 annually.

[DOCUMENT: SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AID]
The university offers a 100% tuition fee waiver Merit Gold Scholarship to students scoring above 95% aggregate in their Class 12 board examinations. To renew this yearly scholarship, students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 8.5 with zero active backlogs. Sibling concessions offer a 15% tuition fee discount for the younger sibling studying concurrently at the institution. State-government post-matric scholarships are fully accepted for SC/ST/OBC category students. Economically Weaker Section (EWS) financial assistance provides a 50% tuition waiver for applicants with a verified family annual income below ₹8,00,000. M.Tech students qualifying through GATE receive a monthly teaching assistantship stipend of ₹12,400. Students are strictly prohibited from combining two different institutional scholarships concurrently.

[DOCUMENT: CAMPUS PLACEMENTS AND INFRASTRUCTURE]
The average placement package offered to Computer Science and Engineering graduates is ₹8.5 Lakhs Per Annum (LPA), with top recruiters including TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, and Amazon. The university campus features 24/7 Wi-Fi connectivity for student laptops. The central library timings are from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekdays, and extended up to 2:00 AM during final semester examination weeks. First-year outstation students are highly encouraged, but not mandatory, to reside in the campus hostels. The university enforces a strict zero-tolerance anti-ragging policy managed by an active campus student grievance cell. Engineering branch changes are permitted after the first year based purely on CGPA merit during counseling if seats are vacant.
